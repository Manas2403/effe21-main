About Us

IIIT A is one of the leading illustrious institutes all over India , countless talented individuals gain experiences & attain unprecedented professional crests in their expedition.
Effervescence is the once-a-year cultural zenith of IIIT Allahabad. It’s one of the most spectacular fests over colleges of North India. This carnival irradiates the essence and ethos of IIITA.
Foremost ,stage events of our festival are enkindled by famed people. Additionally, the assorted number of cultural events of belles-lettres, melody & theatricals embellishes the glowing glamour of the fest .

Effervescence

Effervescence remains an unrivaled platform, one and all showcased their brilliance and relished the time of their online college life. A spectacle of music, dance and arts, EFFE endures and always has been a culture you can ne’er get enough of. Apart from lavish shows like star night with famous artists, the various competitive events were conducted and heavily rewarding cash prizes. Effervescence continues its legacy, getting gigantic, finer and stronger with each passing year.

OUR TEAM

Effervescence is the amalgamation of cultural extravaganza and enthused spirits. Each year, we aim to outdo ourselves and raise the bar even higher. This is possible only through the dedication and sincere efforts of Team Effervescence- a group of like minded and passionate individuals who work together to make each iteration a phenomena in its own.

Our Legacy

Being one of the largest cultural fest among North Indian colleges, Effervescence boasts of having hosted major celebrities and performance from top notch artists, engaging competitions and footfall of thousands. The Effervescence main stage has been graced by artists including Neeti Mohan, Farhan Akhtar, Benny Dayal, comedians such as Anubhav SIngh Bassi, Aakash Gupta and many more.
